## About the Course
This course delves into the complex legal, ethical, and societal challenges that arise in the field of information technology. Through real-world case studies, assignments, and discussions, I will explore topics such as:
- Privacy and data protection
- Intellectual property rights
- Professional and ethical responsibilities
- Regulatory compliance
- The societal impact of emerging technologies

My goal is to reflect on these issues, document my insights, and develop a strong ethical foundation for my future career in cybersecurity.
